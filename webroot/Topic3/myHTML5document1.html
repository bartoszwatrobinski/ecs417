I'm living in Warsaw. Warsaw is a beautiful city with many interesting monuments and other places worth visiting.
Public transport in Warsaw is well organised and it's easy to get to any place in the city despite there are only two underground lines.
Some of the must-see places in Warsaw are Warsaw Old Town and "Lazienki Krolewskie" Park-Palace Complex.
Vistula river flows through Warsaw.
A little disadvantage of living in Warsaw are traffic jams during rush hours, but they are easy to avoid by using public transport which is cheap.
If someone wants to find a silent place to learn, there are many libraries.
